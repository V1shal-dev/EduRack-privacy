## 📖 About EduRack

**EduRack** is a free e-learning Android application developed by **Vishal Patil** that centralizes study materials for students across all academic levels — from primary school to professional competitive examinations. The app provides instant access to notes, PYQs, reference books, lab manuals, and placement resources, entirely free of charge with no subscription required.

Whether you're preparing for board exams, entrance tests like NEET and JEE, or government competitive exams like UPSC and SSC, EduRack brings everything you need to a single, student-friendly platform.
